Frequently Asked Questions

What is an "AI Agent" exactly? expand_more
An AI agent is a large language model (like those built by OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google) equipped with tools such as your calendar, email, file system, and databases. Unlike a simple chatbot that only answers questions, an agent can plan and execute multi-step workflows to achieve a goal. OpenClaw acts as the gateway that connects these powerful models to any number of platforms and services, creating integrations with anything that has an API. The result is a team of specialized digital workers, each focused on a specific part of your workflow.
Do I need to be a tech genius to use this? expand_more
Not at all. We are at a tipping point where natural language can be used to design processes and workflows with AI agents. You interact with your system through familiar interfaces like WhatsApp, Telegram, or Slack, just like messaging a colleague. Early adopters who embrace this technology now will have a significant advantage when AI-powered workflows become mainstream. We handle all the technical complexity so you can focus on what matters.
Is my data safe? expand_more
Absolutely. By owning your own agent orchestration setup, you gain full awareness of where your data flows at every step. Your files, memory, context, and conversation history all live on your hardware. AI model APIs process your prompts, but your personal knowledge base never leaves your machine. This consciousness and awareness around data flow makes for better planning when it comes to privacy. Secure networking via Tailscale, secrets managed through 1Password. You control everything.
How long does a typical setup take? expand_more
A standard setup package typically takes 2 to 4 weeks from kickoff to launch. The timeline can vary depending on equipment purchase and delivery times, the complexity of your integrations, and how quickly we can align on your workflow requirements. We work closely with you throughout the process to keep things moving efficiently.
What tools can you integrate with? expand_more
The possibilities are endless. Any platform with an API can be integrated: Google Workspace, Office 365, Slack, Notion, Shopify, GitHub, Airtable, CRM systems, and hundreds more. Once your agents are running, new integrations can be developed and added continuously as your needs evolve. The open architecture means you are never limited to a pre-defined set of tools. If it has an API, your agents can work with it.
